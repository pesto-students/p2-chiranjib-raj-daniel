Miami Heats advance to playoffs

The Miami Heat are once again back in the Eastern Conference finals.They defeated the Philadelphia 76ers 99-90 Thursday in Game 6 of the conference semifinals. The Heat will face either the Boston Celtics or Milwaukee Bucks in the next round. The Bucks lead the Celtics 3-2 in the best-of-7 series entering Game 6. Forward Jimmy Butler led the Heat with 32 points, eight rebounds and four assists. Guard Max Strus, who had struggled during the postseason, finished with 20 points in the deciding victory.
